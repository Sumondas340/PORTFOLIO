I am a person,
Who is positive about every aspect of life. There are many things I like to do, to see and to experience.

I like to read, write, think, dream, talk & listen. I like to see the sunrise in the morning & moonlight at night, like to listen music flowing on my face, feel the smell of the wind coming from the ocean.

I like to look at the clouds in the sky with a blank mind, like to do experiment when I cannot sleep in the middle of the night. I like flowers in spring, rain in summer, leaves in autumn and snow in winter. I like to sleep early & get up late. I like to be alone & sometimes be surrounded by people. I like country’s peace, delicious food and comfortable shoes. I like good books and romantic movies, the land and the nature. At last, obviously I like to laugh.

This is the brief introduction of myself. If you are interested in knowing more about My Study Life or take a look at My Pictures then scroll down & click on the specific area.
